history

Tata Consultancy Services Limited, initially started as Tata Computer Systems, was founded in 1968 by a division of Tata Sons Limited.[22] Its early contracts included punched card services to sister company TISCO (now Tata Steel), working on an Inter-Branch Reconciliation System for the Central Bank of India,[23] and providing bureau services to Unit Trust of India. In 1975, TCS delivered an electronic depository and trading system called SECOM for a Swiss company SIS SegaInterSettle; it also developed System X for the Canadian Depository System and automated the Johannesburg Stock Exchange.[24] TCS associated with a Swiss partner, TKS Teknosoft, which it later acquired.[25]

life-sciences

Since 2006 TCS signed multi million contracts from american biopharma giants Pfizer, Eli Lilly etc for providing clinical research-data management, biostatistics, and medical writing services.[109][110][111] TCS also provides services to european biopharma giants such as Roche, Novartis etc in the areas of clinical data management, biostatistics, clinical programming, pharmacovigilance, and RWE support towards global drug development efforts.[112][113][114]

employee

TCS is one of the largest private-sector employers in India,[123] and the fourth-largest employer among listed Indian companies (after Indian Railways, Indian Army, and India Post).[124] TCS has crossed more than 600,000 employees as of 8 July 2022.[125][126][127][128][129] The number of non-Indian nationals was 21,282 as of 31 March 2013 (7.7%).[125][130] The employee costs for the FY 2012–13 were US$4.38 billion, which was approx. 38% of the total revenue of the company for that period.[131] In the fiscal year 2012–13, TCS recruited a total of 69,728 new staff, of whom 59,276 were based in India and 10,452 were based in the rest of the world.[125] In the same period, the rate of attrition was 10.6%.[125] The average age of a TCS employee is 28 years.[125] The employee utilization rate, excluding trainees, for the FY 2012–13 was 82%.[125] TCS was the fifth-largest United States visa recipient in 2008 (after Infosys, CTS, Wipro, and Mahindra Satyam).[132][133][134] In 2012, the Tata Group companies, including TCS, were the second-largest recipient of H-1B visas.[135][136]

sponsorship

TCS is the title sponsor for Toronto Waterfront Marathon, London Marathon, Amsterdam Marathon, Mumbai Marathon, Lidingöloppet, and New York City Marathon[139][140] and one of the sponsors of City2Surf, Australian Running Festival, Berlin Marathon, Chicago Marathon, and Boston Marathon.[141] In India, it is the title sponsor of World 10K held in Bangalore every year.[142] TCS is a sponsor of the Indian Premier League team Rajasthan Royals since 2009.[143] Besides, TCS provides Rajasthan Royals with technology to help in the analysis of player performance, simulation, and use of RFID tags for tracking the players’ fitness levels and for security purposes in the stadiums.[144][145] TCS sponsors an annual IT quiz for high school students called TCS IT Wiz.[146] The TCS IT Wiz is the largest quiz in India, attracting students who are studying between class 8 and class 12.
